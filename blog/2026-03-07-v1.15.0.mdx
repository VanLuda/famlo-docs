This release overhauls the checkout experience with an accordion layout, adds Apple Pay and Google Pay support, introduces a letter of intent generator, and includes a funding map tool for finding homeschool resources.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Redesigned Checkout
The checkout flow has been completely redesigned with a clean, step-by-step accordion layout, making it easier to review your order and complete purchases.

### Express Checkout with Apple Pay and Google Pay
You can now pay for events and group memberships using Apple Pay or Google Pay for a faster, more convenient checkout experience.

### Letter of Intent Generator
A new tool helps families generate a letter of intent for homeschooling, pre-filled with your state's requirements and guidelines.

### Funding Map Tool
Discover homeschool funding opportunities in your area with the new interactive funding map, helping you find scholarships, grants, and financial resources.

### Cancel Registration Flow
Event attendees can now cancel their own registrations directly, without needing to contact the group leader.

## Improvements

- Unified navigation header across all pages for a more consistent experience
- Group analytics pages have been reorganized for better clarity
- Subscription lifecycle management for recurring group memberships
- Significant performance improvements with faster data loading across the platform
