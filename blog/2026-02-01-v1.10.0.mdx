This major release introduces subscription refunds with pro-rated calculations, a complete leave group flow, and significant UX improvements across the platform.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Subscription Refunds

When canceling a subscription, you now receive a pro-rated refund for the unused portion. The refund amount is calculated automatically and displayed before you confirm the cancellation.

### Leave Group Flow

Members can now leave groups with a streamlined flow that handles everything automatically. When you leave a group with an active subscription, the system cancels your subscription, processes any applicable refund, and removes your upcoming RSVPs.
