This release introduces a redesigned homepage, a full-width resources mega menu, a content management system for articles and guides, and server-side image optimization across all uploads.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Redesigned Homepage
Updated hero section, trust indicators, "New to Homeschooling" section, and a clearer "How It Works" flow to help new families get started faster.

### Resources Mega Menu
A new full-width mega menu under Resources makes it easy to browse guides, articles, and state-specific homeschool information at a glance.

### Content Management System
Group leaders and administrators can now create and manage blog articles with integrated stock photo search, making it easy to publish helpful content for your community.

### Image Optimization
All uploaded images are now automatically optimized on the server, resulting in faster page loads and reduced data usage across the platform.

## Improvements
