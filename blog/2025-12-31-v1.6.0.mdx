This release introduces geographic discovery pages to help families find local homeschool groups and events by state and city, adds a new age range filter for more targeted browsing, and brings auto-save to group settings.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Geographic Discovery Pages
Find homeschool groups and events in your area with new state and city landing pages. Browse groups in California, events in Texas, or narrow down to specific cities like Austin or Los Angeles. Each page shows relevant groups and events based on location, making it easier to connect with your local homeschool community.

### Age Range Filter
A new slider-based filter lets you browse groups and events by children's ages. Set a range that matches your family and see results tailored to your kids' age groups. Perfect for finding activities appropriate for toddlers, elementary students, teens, or mixed-age groups.

### Auto-Save for Group Settings
Changes to group settings now save automatically as you edit. No more losing work if you navigate away, and no need to remember to click save. The settings page indicates when changes are being saved.
