This release adds full-funnel marketing analytics to help measure and optimize how families discover the platform, along with improved consent handling for returning users.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Full-Funnel Marketing Analytics
Comprehensive conversion tracking now covers the entire user journey, from first visit through event registration and group membership. This helps us better understand which marketing channels are most effective at connecting families with homeschool resources.

---

## Improvements

- Returning users who have already granted consent no longer see repeated consent prompts
- Improved marketing attribution accuracy for better understanding of user acquisition
