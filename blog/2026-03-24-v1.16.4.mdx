This release updates the platform to comply with the 2024 COPPA amendment, strengthening protections for children's data with enhanced parental consent, audit logging, and organizer acknowledgment requirements.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Enhanced Parental Consent
The parental consent verification process has been expanded with more detailed disclosures about what data is collected, how it is used, and who can access it. Parents now receive a confirmation email after verifying consent.

### Organizer Data Handling Acknowledgment
Group leaders who access attendee health information are now presented with a one-time acknowledgment reminding them of their data handling obligations under the platform's terms of service.

---

## Improvements

- Consent verification emails now include comprehensive disclosures about data types and purposes
- Automated cleanup of expired consent records to protect unverified data
- Re-consent notifications are automatically sent when privacy policies are updated
