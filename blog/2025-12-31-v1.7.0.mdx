This release adds dedicated discovery pages for virtual homeschool groups and events, making it easier to find online communities and activities regardless of your location.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Virtual Groups Discovery
Browse homeschool groups that meet online or operate remotely. The new Virtual Groups page showcases communities that offer virtual meetups, online classes, and remote collaboration. Perfect for families in rural areas, those with busy schedules, or anyone looking to connect with homeschoolers beyond their local area.

### Virtual Events Discovery
Find online homeschool events with the new Virtual Events page. Discover webinars, virtual field trips, online classes, and remote meetups. Filter and search through events that you can attend from anywhere.
