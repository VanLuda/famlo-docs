This release introduces infrastructure improvements to keep Famlo running smoothly even when external services experience issues.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Improved Platform Reliability
Famlo now gracefully handles temporary outages from external services. When a service is temporarily unavailable, the platform automatically detects the issue and provides helpful feedback instead of confusing error messages. Once the service recovers, normal operation resumes automatically.

### Admin Monitoring Dashboard
Platform administrators can now monitor system health in real-time with a new dashboard that shows the status of all connected services.

---

## Improvements

- Failed email notifications are now automatically queued and retried when service resumes
- Clearer error messages when external services are temporarily unavailable
