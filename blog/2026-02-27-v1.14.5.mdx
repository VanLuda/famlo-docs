This release introduces content reporting, group moderation tools, user blocking, and a privacy consent system. Discovery pages also receive significant performance improvements.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Content Reporting
Users can now report inappropriate content directly from the platform, helping keep the community safe and welcoming.

### Group Moderation Dashboard
Group leaders now have a dedicated moderation dashboard to review and manage reported content within their groups.

### User Blocking
You can now block other users to prevent unwanted interactions, giving you more control over your experience.

### Privacy Consent
A new privacy consent system respects your browser's privacy preferences and gives you clear control over data sharing.

---

## Improvements

- Discovery pages for groups and events now load significantly faster
- Map clustering now provides a smoother experience when viewing many groups or events in one area
- Faceted filters now show accurate result counts before you apply them
- Events now support language preferences for multilingual communities
- Added skip navigation links for improved keyboard accessibility

---

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed issue where feed content could show events from outside your area
- Fixed past event badges not displaying correctly
