This release brings mobile-optimized bottom sheets for a more native app feel, rebrands Watchlist to Favorites, and improves discovery page performance.

<!-- truncate -->

---

## What's New

### Mobile Bottom Sheets

Notifications and the user menu now open as bottom sheets on mobile devices, providing a more intuitive and native-feeling experience. Swipe down on the handle or tap the backdrop to dismiss.

### Share Modal with Embed Support

The new share modal makes it easy to share groups and events. Copy a direct link, share to social media, or grab an embed code to add Homeschool Hive content to your website.

### Email Subscription Management

Manage your email notification preferences directly from any Homeschool Hive email with a single click.

---

## Improvements

- Watchlist has been rebranded to Favorites for clearer terminology
- Discovery pages now load faster with improved caching
- Event detail page has a modernized layout on mobile
- New events now have discussions enabled by default
- Event cards show ticket information and timezone at a glance

---

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed map not refreshing when switching between light and dark mode
- Resolved browser compatibility issues with error tracking
- Fixed event series display in location-based discovery
