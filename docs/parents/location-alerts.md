# Location Alerts

Location alerts let you know when new groups or events are created near a specific city or area. Whether you are searching for a co-op, a meetup, or classes in a new neighborhood, location alerts save you from checking back manually. You set up a watch once, and Famlo emails you whenever something new matches your criteria.

## How Location Alerts Work

When you create a location alert, you choose:

- **A location** -- the city or area you want to monitor.
- **What to watch for** -- new groups, new events, or both.
- **A search radius** -- how far from that location to look (between 5 and 100 miles; default is 25 miles).

Famlo then monitors that area and sends you an email whenever a matching group or event is created.

:::info
Location alerts expire after **90 days of inactivity**. If no new matches are found in that window, the alert is automatically deactivated. You can always create a new one.
:::

## Creating a Location Alert

### If You Are Logged In

1. Browse to the **Groups** or **Events** page.
2. Use the location search to find your desired area.
3. When prompted, click **Get Notified** or **Create Alert** for that location.
4. Choose whether you want to watch for **Groups**, **Events**, or **Both**.
5. Adjust the radius if you want a wider or narrower search area.
6. Click **Create Alert**.

Because you are already signed in, the alert activates immediately -- no email verification is needed.

### If You Are Not Logged In

You can still set up location alerts without an account:

1. Browse to the **Groups** or **Events** page.
2. Search for a city or area.
3. Click **Get Notified** when prompted.
4. Choose what to watch for (groups, events, or both).
5. Enter your **email address**.
6. Click **Create Alert**.
7. Check your inbox for a **verification email** from Famlo.
8. Click the verification link in the email.

After clicking the link, you will see a confirmation page that reads "You're All Set!" along with the location and alert type you selected.

:::tip
If you already have a Famlo account, consider logging in before creating alerts. Logged-in alerts activate instantly and are easier to manage from your account.
:::

## Verifying Your Email

If you created an alert without being logged in, you must verify your email before the alert becomes active.

- The verification link is sent to the email address you provided.
- The link can only be used **once**. If you click it a second time, you will see an "Invalid or Expired Link" message -- but your alert is already active.
- If you do not receive the email, check your spam or junk folder.

### Verification Errors

If something goes wrong during verification, you may see one of these messages:

| Error | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Invalid or Expired Link** | The link was already used or has expired. If you already verified, your alert is active. |
| **Missing Verification Token** | The link appears incomplete. Try clicking it again from your email. |
| **Something Went Wrong** | A server error occurred. Try again later or contact support. |

## Setting Preferences

After creating an alert, you may be asked to share a few preferences to help Famlo send you more relevant matches:

- **Children's age ranges** -- for example, 0--4, 5--7, 8--10, 11--13, or 14--18.
- **Interests** -- subjects or activities your family enjoys.
- **Preferred group type** -- co-op, meetup, classes, or any.
- **Travel radius preference** -- 10, 25, or 50 miles.

These preferences are optional but help Famlo prioritize the most relevant alerts for your family.

## Managing Your Alerts

If you are logged in, you can view and manage all of your active location alerts:

1. Navigate to your account settings or the location alerts management area.
2. You will see a list of your active watches, including:
   - The location being monitored
   - The alert type (groups, events, or both)
   - The search radius
   - How many matches have been found so far
   - When the alert expires

### Pausing and Resuming Alerts

You can temporarily pause an alert without deleting it:

- **Pause** -- stops email notifications while keeping the alert saved.
- **Resume** -- reactivates notifications.

This is useful if you are traveling or want to temporarily stop receiving emails for a particular area.

### Deleting an Alert

To permanently remove a location alert:

1. Find the alert in your list.
2. Click **Delete** or the trash icon.
3. Confirm the deletion.

Once deleted, the alert cannot be recovered. You would need to create a new one.

## Unsubscribing from Alert Emails

Every alert email includes an **Unsubscribe** link at the bottom. Clicking it will deactivate that specific location alert. This is especially useful for alerts created without an account, where you may not have a management dashboard.

## What Notifications Will I Receive?

When a new group or event is created within your watch radius:

- You receive an **email notification** describing the new group or event.
- The email includes a direct link to view the group or event on Famlo.

:::note
Location alerts send email notifications only. They do not trigger push notifications or in-app alerts.
:::
